My Web Dev Experience


My name is Shaman. I am a 1st year Computer Science Student at College of Engineering Thalassery, Kannur, Kerala. I was a Biology Student in twelth and I was unsure about Computer Science Background. I learnt about '30 Days of Web Development' from my friend. At first, I thought this program wasn't for me but since I was curious I signed up for it.

In the program, we learnt the programming languages HTML, CSS and JavaScript. We also learned about a framework called Bootstrap and a version control called Git. At the end, I learned to host my portfolio website using Firebase.

I never thought that I would learn so many things in just a short period of time. I am glad that I decided to join this program. The mentors guidance and the resources they provided really healped me to understand the concepts. Now that I know how to go about with learning web development, I will continue to brush up my basics and make more meaningful projects.
